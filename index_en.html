Experience & Skills

combining biotechnology with advanced computational tools

Biotechnologist specializing in process optimization and quality management for industrial and research environments.

My focus is on ensuring maximum reliability of results through strict compliance with regulations (ISO) and the implementation of computational tools. I use automation and statistical analysis not only to speed up work but to minimize risks and errors, ensuring full traceability from sample to final report.

  • Data Interpretation: Ability to transform raw data into quality reports through precise statistical analysis.
